RxList is an online medical resource dedicated to offering detailed and current pharmaceutical information on brand and generic drugs.   <!--more--> Founded by pharmacists in 1995, RxList is the premier Internet Drug Index resource.Acquired by WebMD in 2004, our knowledgeable staff at RxList continuously reviews and updates the site with articles written by pharmacists and physicians and data provided by credible and reliable sources like the FDA, Cerner Multum, and First Databank, Inc. to ensure the most accurate and beneficial information is provided.
