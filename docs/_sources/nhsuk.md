We provide a blood and transplantation service to the NHS, looking after blood donation services in England and transplant services across the UK. <!--more-->
 This includes managing the donation, storage and transplantation of blood and blood components, organs, tissues, bone marrow and stem cells, and researching new treatments and processes.

See: <https://www.nhsbt.nhs.uk/who-we-are/>
