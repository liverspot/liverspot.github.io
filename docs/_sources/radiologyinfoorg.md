RadiologyInfo.org tells you how various x-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound, radiation therapy and other procedures are performed. <!--more-->  It also addresses what you may experience and how to prepare for the exams. The website contains about 300 procedure, exam, radiation safety and disease descriptions covering diagnostic and interventional radiology, nuclear medicine, radiation therapy and radiation safety and is updated frequently with new information. All material on the RadiologyInfo.org website is reviewed and approved by experts in the field of radiology from RSNA, ACR, and ASRT as well as other professional radiology organizations.


See: <https://www.radiologyinfo.org>
